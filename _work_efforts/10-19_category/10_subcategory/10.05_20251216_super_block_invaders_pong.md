## Objective
Transform the basic Pong easter egg into "Super Block Invaders Pong" - an epic mashup of Pong, Breakout, and Space Invaders with lives, powerups, space invaders, brick walls, and a comprehensive scoring system.

## Game Design Vision

### Core Concept
A hybrid arcade game where:
- Two players (you vs CPU) defend their brick walls using paddles
- Space Invaders float in the middle zone and shoot lasers at both players
- Ball bounces and destroys bricks/invaders
- Powerups drop from destroyed bricks/invaders
- Lives system - get hit by lasers = lose life
- Win by: destroying all opponent bricks OR reducing their lives to 0

## Features to Implement

### 1. Brick Walls (Breakout-style)
- [ ] 4x5 grid of bricks on each side protecting the goals
- [ ] Different brick types: normal (1 hit), strong (2 hits), indestructible
- [ ] Bricks have colors based on strength
- [ ] Brick destruction triggers particle effects

### 2. Space Invaders
- [ ] 2-3 rows of invaders floating in the middle
- [ ] Invaders move in classic formation (left-right, descend)
- [ ] Random shooting (lasers go toward both players)
- [ ] Invaders respawn in waves with increasing difficulty
- [ ] Different invader types with different behaviors

### 3. Lives System
- [ ] Player starts with 3 lives
- [ ] CPU has 3 lives
- [ ] Lose life when: hit by invader laser, ball passes your paddle
- [ ] Game over when lives = 0
- [ ] Lives displayed as hearts

- Destroy brick: +100 points
- Destroy strong brick: +200 points
- Destroy invader: +150 points

- Win round: +1000 points
- Combo multiplier for rapid hits
